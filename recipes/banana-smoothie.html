Banana-Blueberry Spinach Smoothie

Overview

A healthy smoothie suited for everyday mornings. All produce items can be bought frozen and with no taste of any spinach.
  • Prep Time: 1 min
  • Cook Time: <1 min
  • Total Time: 2 min
  • Servings: 1 person

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 100g of fresh or frozen chopped spinach
  • 60g of frozen blueberries
  • 60g of greek yogurt
  • 100ml milk
  • 1 pinch of flax seeds

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients into a Nutri-Bullet blender bottle.
  2. Blend all ingredients together until smooth. Add more milk if a thinner smoothie is desired.
  3. Enjoy!
